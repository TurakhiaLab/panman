Sars-CoV 2 (4k) Dataset


Use minimap2 to align and save as SAM


minimap2 -a -t  8 ~/localdata/sars/sars_4k_r.fa ~/localdata/sars/sars_4k_n-1.fa > sar_4k.sam



Sam to bam
samtools view -S -b sar_4k.sam > sar_4k.bam


bam to sam
samtools view -h sar_4k.bam > sar_4k.sam


CRAM is primarily a reference-based compressed format, meaning that only differences between the stored sequences and the reference are stored.


Bam to cram
samtools view -C -T ~/localdata/sars/sars_4k_r.fa sar_4k.bam > sar_4k.cram


Cram to bam
samtools view -b -T ~/localdata/sars/sars_4k_r.fa -o file.bam sar_4k.cram
